Brandy M. Miller

Author, Artist, Designer, Developer, Marketer, International Speaker, & Founder of 40 Day Writer LLC and Project FED

Brandy M. Miller is a mutlti-talented international speaker and award-winning author and artist whose interests cover game design and development, web design and development, graphic, fabric, and fashion design, as well as entrepreneurship and marketing.

She is the founder of 40 Day Writer LLC, where she helps individuals get their stories out of their heads and into the hands of readers, as well as Project FED where she helps low and no-income earners make money online promoting products that support orphans in Nigeria.
